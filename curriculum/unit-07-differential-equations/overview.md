# Unit 7: Differential Equations

## Description
Students solve differential equations, interpret and sketch slope fields, and apply differential equations to model real-world phenomena including exponential growth, decay, and logistic growth.

## Major Topics
- Modeling with differential equations
- Verifying solutions to differential equations
- Slope fields
- Euler's method (numerical approximation)
- Separation of variables
- Exponential growth and decay models
- Logistic growth (conceptual overview)

## AP Exam Relevance
Differential equations appear in AP FRQ questions almost every year. Students must be able to interpret slope fields, solve separable DEs, and apply exponential models.

## Evidence of Mastery
Students can:
- Verify that a function is a solution to a differential equation
- Sketch a slope field from a given differential equation
- Use separation of variables to solve a separable differential equation
- Apply an initial condition to find a particular solution
- Model exponential growth and decay using dy/dt = ky
- Interpret a logistic model qualitatively
- Use Euler's method to approximate solutions numerically

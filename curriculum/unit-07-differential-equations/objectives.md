# Unit 7: Differential Equations — Learning Objectives

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:

1. Define a differential equation and identify the order of a DE.
2. Verify that a given function satisfies a differential equation.
3. Interpret the meaning of a differential equation in a real-world context.
4. Sketch a slope field given a differential equation dy/dx = f(x, y).
5. Match a differential equation to its slope field.
6. Determine the behavior of a solution curve from a slope field.
7. Use Euler's method to approximate a solution with a given step size.
8. Identify differential equations that can be solved by separation of variables.
9. Solve separable differential equations to find a general solution.
10. Apply initial conditions to find particular solutions.
11. Model and solve exponential growth and decay problems using dy/dt = ky.
12. Interpret the logistic differential equation and describe its solution qualitatively.

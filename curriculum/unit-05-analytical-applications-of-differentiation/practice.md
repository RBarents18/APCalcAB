# Unit 5: Analytical Applications of Differentiation — Practice

## Starter Practice Problems

### Mean Value Theorem
1. Verify that the Mean Value Theorem applies to f(x) = x² - 4x + 1 on [0, 3] and find the value(s) of c guaranteed by the theorem.
2. Explain why the MVT does or does not apply to f(x) = |x| on [-2, 2].

### Increasing and Decreasing Behavior
3. For f(x) = 2x³ - 9x² + 12x, find the intervals where f is increasing and where f is decreasing.
4. A function f has f′(x) = (x - 1)²(x + 3). Determine the intervals of increase and decrease.

### Critical Points and Local Extrema
5. Find all critical points of f(x) = x⁴ - 4x³ and classify each using the first derivative test.
6. Use the second derivative test to classify the critical points of f(x) = x³ - 6x² + 9x + 2.

### Concavity and Inflection Points
7. Determine intervals of concavity and inflection points for f(x) = x⁴ - 6x².
8. Given the graph of f″, sketch the shape of f.

### Global Extrema
9. Find the global maximum and minimum of f(x) = 2x³ - 3x² - 12x + 4 on [-2, 3].
10. A function is continuous on [0, 5] with f(0) = 3, f(2) = 8, f(4) = 1, f(5) = 6. Identify the global max and min.

### Optimization
11. A farmer wants to enclose a rectangular area of 400 m² using fencing along three sides (the fourth side is a barn). Find the dimensions that minimize the total length of fencing.
12. A box with a square base and no top must have a volume of 32 cubic inches. Find the dimensions that minimize the surface area.

## Activity Ideas
- Sign chart station: build f′ sign charts from different types of functions
- Curve sketching synthesis: given derivative info, sketch f
- Optimization design challenge: maximize area or minimize cost in a real context
- AP FRQ: given f′ graph, justify behavior of f (classic AP format)

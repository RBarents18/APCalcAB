# Unit 4: Contextual Applications of Differentiation — Skills

## AP Calculus AB Skill Categories

### Skill 1: Implementing Mathematical Processes
- Set up and solve related rates problems using an appropriate geometric or applied equation
- Apply implicit differentiation with respect to time
- Construct and use a linearization: L(x) = f(a) + f′(a)(x - a)
- Apply L'Hôpital's Rule: lim f(x)/g(x) = lim f′(x)/g′(x) when form is 0/0 or ∞/∞

### Skill 2: Connecting Representations
- Connect position, velocity, and acceleration as successive derivatives
- Translate a word problem into a mathematical diagram and equation
- Interpret rate of change in unfamiliar applied contexts (economics, science, engineering)

### Skill 3: Justification
- Justify whether a particle is speeding up or slowing down using signs of v and a
- Verify that L'Hôpital's Rule is applicable before using it
- Justify total distance versus net displacement with supporting calculations

### Skill 4: Communication and Notation
- Label rates with correct units (e.g., cm/min, ft/sec²)
- Write related rates solutions with clearly identified variables, diagram, and steps
- Communicate meaning of a rate of change in a full sentence appropriate for an FRQ

## Key Vocabulary
- Rate of change, related rates, implicit differentiation with respect to time
- Position, velocity, acceleration, speed, displacement, total distance
- Linearization, local linear approximation, tangent line approximation
- L'Hôpital's Rule, indeterminate form
